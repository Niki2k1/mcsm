Exploring new terrain is the single biggest source of lag on a Minecraft
server — every new chunk has to be generated on the fly. The **World** tab
fixes that by generating chunks ahead of time with
[Chunky](https://modrinth.com/mod/chunky).

## Enable pre-generation

Click **Enable** and MCSM installs the right Chunky build for your server's
loader and Minecraft version from Modrinth. Works on Paper, Fabric, Forge and
CurseForge modpack servers.

## Start a pre-generation run

Pick a **radius** (in blocks) around a center point and start. Chunky works
through the area while the server runs:

- **Live progress** — completion percentage, generation rate and ETA.
- **Chunk colormap** — a Minecraft-style top-down map fills in as chunks are
  generated, so you can literally watch your world take shape.
- **Pause / resume / cancel** — pre-generation is throttled by the server
  itself, and you stay in control.

::tip
Pre-generate at least the area inside your world border before inviting
players — a few hours of pre-generation saves weeks of intermittent lag.
::

## Requirements

::note
Chunky needs **Minecraft 1.13.2 or newer**. On CurseForge modpacks, the
matching Chunky build is resolved against the modpack's mod loader — packs on
older Minecraft versions have no compatible build.
::
